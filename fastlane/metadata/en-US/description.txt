OAEC 57th Legislature Guide is a digital resource of Oklahoma’s federal and state elected officials. This application allows users to interactively track their voting roll call predictions, as well as share those predictions. Here users find features such as live phone numbers, e-mail, website and social media links as well as an interactive map that showcases counties and legislative districts. Everything you need to know about Oklahoma’s 57th Legislature at your fingertips. Brought to you by the Oklahoma Association of Electric Cooperatives.
